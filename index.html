[x] About

Hi there, I'm Asif Sayyed.

I'm a problem solver and Data Science Specialist at Marsh McLennan, focused on turning raw data into clear, useful solutions. My background is in Data Science & Business Analytics, with professional experience in the insurance industry analyzing cyber risk.

I draw inspiration from the strategist Sun Tzu, who wrote, "In the midst of chaos, there is also opportunity." To me, data is that chaos. My work is to find the opportunity hidden within it.

What truly excites me isn't just analyzing data; it's building with it. I use tools like Python, SQL, and Databricks to create working applications that turn abstract numbers into something tangible that anyone can use.

Whether you're a fellow developer or just curious, I invite you to explore my portfolio.
