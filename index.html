About me

Asif Sayyed is a Data Science Specialist at Marsh McLennan's Cyber Risk Intelligence Center in Mumbai, where he specializes in turning complex data into clear, actionable solutions for the insurance industry. With a comprehensive background in Data Science and Business Analytics, Asif leverages tools like Python, SQL, and Databricks to model cyber risks and build robust, data-driven applications. He draws inspiration from the strategist Sun Tzu, who wrote, "In the midst of chaos, there is also opportunity." Approaching raw data as that chaos, Asif's work focuses on uncovering the opportunities hidden within it. Moving beyond standard analysis, he actively builds scalable solutions that translate complex numbers into practical tools. If you are a developer, recruiter, or simply curious about data science, feel free to browse his portfolio and explore his open-source projects.
