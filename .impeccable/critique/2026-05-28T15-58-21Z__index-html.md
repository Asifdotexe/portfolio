## Anti-Patterns Verdict

Not AI-generated. Passes the AI slop test. Custom color system, typewriter effect, tilt-on-hover, staggered entrance animations, and project data pipeline show genuine craft. No glassmorphism, no gradient text, no hero-metrics, no identical card grids. Detector scan unavailable (script not bundled).

## Overall Impression

A confident, no-nonsense portfolio that puts evidence before decoration. Content quality is high with real project data, JSON-LD structured data, and good performance practices.

## Priority Issues

- **[P1] Navbar overflow**: flex-wrap caused items to stack unpredictably on small viewports. Fixed.
- **[P2] Form feedback is fragile**: Submit redirects away, no inline success/error, disabled button without explanation.
- **[P2] No keyboard navigation**: 18 projects, 6 sections require click-through every tab.
- **[P3] Copy polish**: Lowercase "i" in "What i'm currently doing?", awkward question mark in bio.
- **[P3] Map embed inline styles**: border-radius and box-shadow hardcoded on iframe.

## Persona Red Flags

**Alex (Power User)**: No keyboard shortcuts, must click through tabs, 18 projects require scrolling.

**Priya (Recruiter)**: Scanning slowly through 4-paragraph bio before reaching concrete skills.

**Jordan (First-Timer)**: Dark theme may feel heavy, no onboarding guidance on first click.
